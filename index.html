Meet our faciltators

Kunle-toba Dada

Kunle-toba Dada studied Theatre, Music, T.V & Film Production at the Lagos State University. Having worked on shows like BBC's Story-story radio drama, Wetin Dey TV series, MNet's Battleground & Tinsel, he's a vastly experienced Dramatist, Story Developer, Teacher and Writer.

Nne Uchechukwu Nlemadim

Nne Uchechukwu Nlemadim is a graduate of Theatre Arts from the University of Nigeria Nsukka. She was formerly a lighting designer and Operator with the popular TV show “Who Wants to be a Millionaire?” and “Project Fame West Africa”. She also worked in Zain/Airtel as a Contact Solutions Provider for 3 years. Nne moved into the movie industry on the platform of Africa Magic Original Films (AMOF) where she excelled as a Production Coordinator and Production Manager. She was also a Line Producer and Head of Production for Native Media. In addition, she served as Line Producer for the Africa Magic TV series “Jemeji”. Nne is also a freelancer, working as a Line Producer/Associate Producer. Nne Nlemadim is also a freelance script writer. She has written for BBC’s “Story Story”, the TV series “So Wrong, So Wright”, “Ajoche” and rewritten some uncredited scripts for AMOF.

Ogochukwu Sylvia Uzoma

Ogochukwu Sylvia Uzoma started her university education in Enugu State University of Science and Technology studying Banking and Finance and switched over to National Open University of Nigeria where she got her Degree in Mass Communication.
